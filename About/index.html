Our Mission

We aim to make games for the players. Community feedback is a vital part

of our development process and we want the fans to feel as responsible

for the creation of the games they enjoy as we do.

The Team

Hi, I'm Joe, a computer science undergraduate from the UK. I created

Pixole Studios at the end of 2018 as a personal project to form a studio

and release games. I've been developing games in my spare time for a few

years, mostly just small projects and jam entries, but I hope to soon

release a full game under Pixole Studios.
