Who?

Pixole Studios is a "small team" currently consisting of just me, Joe Rickard, a computer science undergrad.

What?

Pixole Studios is a game development studio, normally working on several projects at any given time. Some will be publicly shared as prototypes on my itch.io page, other larger projects are worked on in the background...

When?

Pixole Studios started in 2018 and is worked on when I'm not at work or studying and in any spare time I have. We have no releases planned as of yet but watch this space...

Where?

Why?
