ABOUT ME

I'm Joe Rickard, an undergraduate software engineer from the UK. I am currently studying for my degree in Computer Science at the University of Reading. I have a passion for creating practical software that people will actually use and enjoy using. This is also why I have ventured into game development in my free time, starting my own game studio "Pixole Studios" at the end of 2018.
